The Data, Systems and HPC (DaSH) Lab at BITS Pilani, K. K. Birla Goa Campus is led by Dr. Arnab K. Paul. DaSH Lab's research is in the field of data storage technologies, edge computing, optimizing systems for machine learning workloads as well as using machine learning to optimize systems, and high performance computing.

We are always looking for motivated undergraduate, graduate, and PhD students to join our team. Motivated individuals are requested to check our Join Us page.
